About Actress

Ester Noronha is an artist/entertainer, well known as a singer, dancer, performer and film actress in Konkani, Telugu and Kannada languages so far. She is currently venturing into Hindi, Tamil and Marathi film industries with one project in each of these above mentioned languages. Ester Noronha was born to Valerian Noronha and Janet Noronha in Bahrain. Due to the interest Ester showed in music and dance at a very young age, her mother gave up her career and came down to Mangalore along with Ester, where Ester continued her schooling while simultaneously pursuing training in piano, Bharatnatyam, Carnatic singing, guitar and also performing in live shows in Konkani language at the age of 9. She then became very popular as a child artist and performer among the Mangalorean Konkani community and went on to perform in numerous shows locally and abroad, delivering several hit numbers and participating in albums and shows of all the top composers and musicians in Konkani language.

Ester was brought up in Mangalore between years 2000-2008. She attended St. Gerosa school, Jeppu, Mangalore between 3rd to 10th std during which she accomplished quite a few things in the field of arts too. She completed 8 grades in Piano from the Trinity college of London, Senior grade in Bharatnatyam from the KSEEB (Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board) And also performed the 'Rangapravesha 'in Bharatnatyam.

She completed the junior grade in Carnatic singing from the KSEEB and also performed the "Rangapravesha" in the same. Ester is the first artist in Dakshina Kannada district to have performed Rangapravesha in both Bharatnatyam and Carnatic singing. At the age of 14, she established her independent musical live show with the title "Ester Show" which was in aid of the Mangalore Diocesan Medical relief fund.

Ester was a bright student academically and passed the 10th std with a distinction. Due to her interest in the social sciences and her drive to do something for the society, she dreamed on getting into civil services for which she planned on pursuing her studies in Arts. She along with her mother then shifted to Mumbai where she joined the prestigious St. Xaviers college under the merit list. She went on to Graduate as a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and Psychology subjects.
Even as she shifted to Mumbai she continued to keep training in music and dance and shows whenever possible. During the college phase she got trained in Hindustani singing under Ustad Hafiz Khan, Mumbai. She produced and released her maiden audio album in Konkani titled 'Ester's "Kalza Thaun"(from the heart)' comprising 10 songs composed and sung by herself at the age of 16.
It was during one of her Bharatnatyam shows in Bangalore, that the legendary Bollywood choreographer Saroj Khan laid her eyes upon Ester. She noticed the talent and the potential in her and advised her to try her luck in films. Ester went on to get trained in Kathak and Bollywood dancing under Saroj Khan for around 2 yrs during which she got introduced to the world of movie making and related people. Ester then joined Anupam Kher's 'Actor Prepares',the school of acting where she completed her Diploma course in acting.

Career

Ester was initially hesitant about getting into films as an actor as that field was yet to be explored by her. During this time was when her Guru, Saroj Khan offered her to work in a non- commercial Hindi movie "Baromas" she herself was choreographing. Ester, along with her mother's support and her Master ji's insistence took it up. And from then on there was no looking back. Baromas went on to be played and nominated in various countries and film festivals around the world including the 2012 South Asian International Film Festival in New York and the DCIFF (DC Independent Film Festival) in Washington, DC. It was the only Indian movie to be selected and competing in feature films category against films from USA, Switzerland, UK and Canada. She was then casted by the renowned Director Teja in his Telugu movie "1000 Abaddalu" in 2013 which went on to be a hit and Ester was immediately signed by the prestigious 'Suresh Productions' for their Golden jubilee project "Bhimavaram Bullodu" opposite Hero Sunil Verma. The project was in celebration of the 50th year of the production house and it turned out to be a massive hit completing 50 successful days in theaters across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana thus making Ester Noronha popular and loved by the audience in the Telugu film industry (Tollywood).

Education

Ester has always continued her education and is currently pursuing 2nd year in M.A in Political Science under the University of Mumbai.

Awards

Ester has been conferred the "Kala Rathna" award by Githanjali institution, Mangalore for excellence in fine arts in 2015.
